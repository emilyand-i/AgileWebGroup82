Welcome to Plantly!

Plantly is your personal plant care assistant, built to help you nurture your plants and track their growth over time. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting your plant journey, Plantly gives you the tools to stay connected with your green companions in a meaningful and organised way.

With Plantly, you can easily upload photos of your plants, document their progress, and visualise how they've grown with interactive charts. Record important updates like watering dates and height measurements to monitor each plant’s development. Our platform makes plant care less of a chore and more of a story.

What makes Plantly unique is the balance between functionality and community. Not only can you manage multiple plants with ease, but you can also connect with other users, share your progress, and discover how others care for their gardens. Whether you’re growing a single succulent or managing a lush outdoor garden, Plantly scales with you.

Happy Growing!
